What's new at Amity Kolkata?

Admission 2023 is open for UG and PG programs. Candidates can apply online on the Amity Kolkata official website (amity.edu). Further, Amity JEE 2023 exam date will be announced soon.

Amity University, Kolkata, offers over 20 courses under several streams, including Engineering, Science, Arts, Commerce, Management, Design, Law, and many more. The university accepts applications for all the courses online. Candidates, fulfilling the eligibility criteria, need to submit the application form by visiting the admission portal of the university. Admission to various courses is merit-based or entrance exam-based. Apart from the full-time courses, Amity University Kolkata also offers distance learning via its Online and Distance Learning schools.
